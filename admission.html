Admission Requirements

school

Bachelor's Degree

A bachelor's degree from an accredited institution in any field. Minimum GPA of 3.0 (or equivalent) is recommended.

work_history

Work Experience

Minimum 2 years of full-time professional work experience (recommended). Fresh graduates with exceptional profiles may be considered.

quiz

GMAT/GRE

GMAT (recommended 600+) or GRE (recommended 310+). Waivers may be available for candidates with significant work experience or exceptional academic records.

translate

English Proficiency

TOEFL iBT 90+ or IELTS 6.5+ (for non-native English speakers). Waivers available for degrees from English-speaking institutions.
