Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a campus visit?

You can schedule a campus visit by emailing us at gmba@thu.edu.tw or calling +886-4-2359-0121. We offer both in-person and virtual campus tours.

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. During peak admission periods, response times may be slightly longer.

Can I speak with a current student or alumnus?

Yes! We can arrange for you to connect with current students or alumni. Please mention this in your inquiry and we'll coordinate a conversation.
