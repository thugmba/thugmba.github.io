About the Global MBA Program

In view of the increasingly competitive and closely integrated international business landscape, the demand for international management talent both domestically and abroad has significantly increased. Since its founding, Tunghai University has been committed to creating a quality international learning environment. To meet the growing demand from both Taiwanese and international students for an internationally-oriented, English-taught business administration master's degree, the College of Management at Tunghai University has integrated resources from six departments within the college and the university's international education resources to launch the fully English-taught "Global MBA Program."

The College of Management at Tunghai University emphasizes several key development priorities for the Global MBA: combined classes of local and international students, instruction entirely in English with emphasis on international activities, and diverse learning programs in partnership with sister schools. The program provides multifaceted learning in management theory, cross-cultural literacy, and foreign language communication skills, training students with international business management capabilities, broadening their global perspective, and enhancing their international competitive advantage. Through the integration of solid theoretical foundations with practical workplace applications, we cultivate outstanding international business management and leadership talent.

Program Features

Interdisciplinary Professional Learning

The curriculum and research areas of this degree program emphasize interdisciplinary professional learning for the globalized generation, encouraging future leaders with diverse backgrounds in business or non-business education and multicultural or work experience to expand their practical application and management capabilities in multinational industries through in-depth interactive teaching and case analysis.

Fast Track Option

The program offers flexible curriculum design with a Fast Track option to earn a master's degree early. Students can complete all coursework and their master's thesis in as little as one year through early admission, intensive courses, and overseas research opportunities.

International Elite Teaching Team

This degree program collaborates with the College of Management's International Elite Program to establish an international elite teaching team. In addition to hiring professional faculty with doctoral degrees from top universities across various regions at home and abroad, the program has recruited several American and French professors to teach core English-language courses, providing students with a learning environment comparable to studying abroad.

Future Development

Through English-taught business and management professional courses, GMBA cultivates bilingual talent with global market competitiveness. Supplemented with Chinese language learning resources, the program attracts outstanding international students from Europe, America, and Southeast Asia to study in Taiwan, interact and learn with local students, and cultivate exceptional global leaders in a cross-national, cross-cultural, and interdisciplinary professional learning environment.
