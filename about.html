Our History

Tunghai University's Global MBA program was established with a vision to create a distinctive business education experience that bridges Eastern and Western business practices. Located in Taichung, Taiwan, our program benefits from the region's strategic position in the Asia-Pacific economic landscape.

Over the years, we have built strong partnerships with leading corporations and academic institutions worldwide, creating unique opportunities for our students to engage with global business challenges. Our diverse faculty brings expertise from around the world, enriching the learning experience with varied perspectives and real-world insights.

Today, our alumni network spans across continents, with graduates holding leadership positions in multinational corporations, successful startups, and influential organizations. The GMBA program continues to evolve, incorporating cutting-edge business trends and maintaining our commitment to developing responsible global business leaders.
